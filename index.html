Jokemaniacs

App that lets you create memes and send them instantly to your friends.

A lot of teenagers are depressed and we gotta cheer each other up in some ways.

Try it out!

Features
Improvisation Creativity Custom pictures Custom text
You can do things on the fly, on yourown or with some help. There are no limits, do what you want to! You can add yourown pictures or use ours. Tired on some captions? Add your own ones.
